Workshop Goals

This workshop is organized by the Department of Machine Learning, Computational Biology, and Statistics and Data Science at MBZUAI, in collaboration with the Department of Biostatistics at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. It aims to bring together researchers from diverse disciplines who are interested in statistical analysis, machine learning, and their applications to real-world problems in biology. The event will serve as a platform to share state-of-the-art methods, exchange perspectives, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and identify key challenges and opportunities at the intersection of machine learning, statistical data science, and biological research.
